# WatchGuard Full Encryption

CIS Controls v8 safeguard mappings for **WatchGuard Full Encryption**.

| CIS Safeguard | Safeguard Title | Alignment | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3.6 | Encrypt Data on End-User Devices | Validates; Full |  |
| 3.9 | Encrypt Data on Removable Media | Full | WatchGuard Full Encryption allows encrypting removable storage media on Windows devices. |
| 3.11 | Encrypt Sensitive Data at Rest | Validates; Full | WatchGuard Full Encryption leverages BitLocker (Windows) and FileVault (macOS), proven and stable technologies, to encrypt disks and USB drives without impacting end users.
